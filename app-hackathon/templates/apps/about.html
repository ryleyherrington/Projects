Goal

The long term goal is to help undergrads in EECS to create product prototypes; this includes mobile apps, web apps, desktop apps, hardware-based, or even hybrids of the above.

About the Project

This is the work of 3 Computer Science students studying at Oregon State University. We wanted to provide a way for people to submit all of their ideas in a centralized place and then get a prototype of those ideas out in the world.

The idea is that anyone can have the next big app or game or program idea but not have a way to implement it. This is a way for us to bring multiple fields of study into one place and hopefully produce some outstanding new apps.

For the first part of this project we will have the students submit their ideas and vote on projects. At the end of the term the projects with the most votes will be chosen for the implementation phase.

We will be having teams of about four programmers start to make the prototypes in Winter term. They will be presented in Spring term at the Kelley Engineering Center.
