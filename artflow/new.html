Art Flow

Welcome to Artflow

Artflow is an innovative, rotational artwork exchange designed from the beginning to fit the needs of professional business offices. We are located and serve the Portland, Oregon metropolitan area and are looking to expand to other citiesâ€™ offices looking to keep their professional spaces fresh and different.

We work with local artists to reproduce original artwork in the local Portland area. Our artists are extremely important to Artflow and we work with our artists to create stunning imagery and impactful statements in your professional office spaces.

Our art is sent to our clients who will display local artists in their offices for a fresh, invigorating workspace. When the term is up (3, 6, 9 months), they send back the art and we send them new ones. If our clients love the art so much, they can even purchase it from the artist themselves.

In the end, we help local artists sell their art and make some money for letting us borrow their creativity while helping local professional offices periodically refresh their workspaces with a rotating exhibit.
